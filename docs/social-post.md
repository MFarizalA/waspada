## LinkedIn (long-form, EN)

> **WASPADA — a score you can argue with.**
>
> Most credit-risk models hand you a number. Ours hands you an *argument*.
>
> WASPADA is a multi-agent decision-support system for loan-risk collections, built for the Qwen
> Cloud (Alibaba) hackathon. A classical model scores the whole book — then a **society of six AI
> agents debates the riskiest accounts** before a human acts:
>
> 🔎 a Skeptic audits a stratified slice and challenges the model
> 📊 the Actuary defends its score from its *own* drivers (not a guess)
> ⚖️ an Arbiter rules — or escalates to a human on low confidence
>
> Every claim cites evidence. Every run leaves an audit trail. The human sets the policy and holds
> the final gate.
>
> Under the hood: Qwen (tiered flash/plus/max by cognitive load) for reasoning, a calibrated +
> version-tracked PD model with drift monitoring, and a human "parameter matrix" that governs the
> whole society — all on Alibaba Cloud (OSS · Function Compute · RDS · SLS), fully reproducible and
> offline-testable. One engine, two lanes: **collections** early-warning and **origination**
> approve/refer/reject.
>
> 🎥 Demo: [link] · 💻 Code (MIT): https://github.com/MFarizalA/waspada
>
> #AgentSociety #QwenCloud #AlibabaCloud #AIagents #CreditRisk #MultiAgent #Fintech
