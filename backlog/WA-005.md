# WA-005 · Risk model (cuML, GPU)

## Goal
The GPU scoring step: train a cuML classifier on the `FeatureFrame` to predict
**P(eventual charge-off / default)** (the cross-sectional `label_default` from
WA-004), and emit `ScoredAccounts` (without ranking — that's WA-006). GPU via
WSL.

## Files / interfaces
- `waspada/model/risk.py`:
  - `train(features: FeatureFrame) -> GPUModel` — cuML classifier (start with
    RandomForest or LogisticRegression — pick what fits 4 GB VRAM). **Vintage
    split**: train on older origination vintages (`issue_date`), validate/test
    on newer vintages — an out-of-time-ish check that prevents the model being
    evaluated on the same cohort distribution it trained on, and keeps
    outcome-derived fields out of features.
  - `predict(model, features) -> ScoredAccounts` — attaches `p_default`,
    `score_band` (quintiles); `segment`/`recommended_action` filled by WA-006.
- `gpu/run_model.py` — WSL entry: train + predict, persist artifact under
  `models/` (gitignored), write scored parquet.
- `tests/test_model.py` — output shape, probabilities ∈ [0,1], no NaN scores;
  AUC > 0.5 on a separable toy set.

## Acceptance
- `predict()` returns one row per account, `p_default ∈ [0,1]`, no NaNs.
- Vintage split implemented; a test documents that train/test `issue_date`
  windows don't overlap and that no outcome-derived field is a feature.
- Model artifact saved to `models/` (gitignored); re-runnable.
- VRAM-safe model choice documented.

## Out of scope
Ranking/work-list (WA-006), benchmark (WA-007), tuning beyond a sane default,
calibration (QA may flag — WA-012).

## Depends on
WA-001 (`ScoredAccounts`, `wsl.run_gpu`), WA-004 (`FeatureFrame`,
`label_default`).
