# WA-092 · Adjust the login (AuthScreen) to the cloud-blue theme

**Candidates:** Claude or frontend. Small, contained — closes the theme gap the blue re-theme
leaves on the **first screen every judge/user sees**.

## Why
The blue re-theme (tokens.css) flows through most of `AuthScreen` because it references tokens
(`--cn-red-*` → blue, `--cn-gold-*` → sky). **But the login has hardcoded raw colors that do NOT
re-theme** and will now clash against the blue brand:

- `AuthScreen.module.css:9` — background glow `rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.18)` = **imperial gold**, still
  gold behind a blue login.
- `AuthScreen.module.css:254` / `:258` — dark-mode background glow + tint `rgba(13, 148, 136, …)` =
  **old teal**, a color no longer in the palette.
- `AuthScreen.module.css:177` — `color: #ffffff` (verify it's text-on-colored; likely fine, but audit).
- `AuthScreen.tsx:41` — `favicon.svg` brand mark: confirm it isn't a red/gold glyph that fights the
  blue (swap or recolor if so).

## Design
- **Replace the hardcoded glows with tokens.** Login background radial-gradients should use the blue
  brand + sky accent (`var(--cn-red-50)` / a `color-mix` off `--cn-gold-500`), not raw gold/teal
  rgba. Dark mode uses the blue dark tokens, not teal.
- **Audit every raw hex/rgba in `AuthScreen.module.css`** and route through `tokens.css` so the login
  re-themes with the palette like the rest of the app (the "components reference tokens, never raw
  hex" rule this file currently breaks).
- **Brand mark / favicon:** ensure the logo reads on blue; recolor to the cloud-blue brand or a
  neutral if it's currently red/gold.
- **Typography:** the login inherits `--font-display` (now sans) — sanity-check the title/tagline
  still look right without the serif.
- Keep the layout; this is a **color/asset pass**, not a redesign.

## Acceptance
- No raw gold/teal (or any hardcoded brand hex) remains in `AuthScreen.module.css`; all colors come
  from `tokens.css`.
- Login renders coherently blue in **both light and dark** mode (no gold/teal glow).
- Brand mark/favicon reads cleanly on the blue background.
- Error/validation states, the demo-creds hint, and the language toggle all still legible (contrast
  AA), EN + 简体中文.

## Out of scope
- Auth logic / copy changes — visual only.
- The broader flow-chart view (WA-091) — separate ticket.

## Notes
- Ship on the **same branch/PR as the tokens.css re-theme** so the theme lands complete (no split
  where the app is blue but the login still glows gold).
