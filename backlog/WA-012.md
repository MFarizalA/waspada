# WA-012 · QA — Collections lane quality gate

## Goal
Independent quality gate across the built Collections lane: data validation,
model sanity, pipeline integration, and the **data-leak check** (no
outcome-derived information in training features; vintage-split integrity).
**Advisory** — Reza reports findings; Stefanie routes; owner signs off. Never
gates.

## Files / interfaces
- `tests/qa/test_data_validation.py` — schema/null/range checks on `RawLoans`
  and `FeatureFrame`.
- `tests/qa/test_model_sanity.py` — AUC/calibration on the vintage test split;
  flags a model no better than prior.
- `tests/qa/test_leakage.py` — asserts `label_default` (eventual charge-off /
  default, from final `current_status`) is never derivable from a feature; the
  label column and outcome-derived fields (e.g. post-outcome payment totals) are
  excluded from the feature set. Train/test vintage split (by `issue_date`) must
  not leak newer-vintage outcomes into training.
- `tests/qa/test_pipeline_integration.py` — drives ingest→features→model→
  ranking on a fixture; asserts the `DashboardPayload` validates.
- `tests/qa/REPORT.md` — Reza's findings: pass/fail per check, severity, repro.

## Acceptance
- All four check files run and report pass/fail with evidence.
- Leakage check documents the cross-sectional leakage rules it enforces (no
  outcome-derived features; vintage-split integrity).
- `REPORT.md` lists findings with severity; no blocker hides.
- QA runs against real artifacts from WA-004..006 where feasible (not only toy
  data).

## Out of scope
Fixing bugs (route findings to the owning ticket), new features, Origination
QA, the benchmark (WA-007 is self-tested).

## Depends on
WA-002..006 (the artifacts under test). Dispatch after the pipeline lands.
